About Pollution Near Me

Pollution Near Me is a public-facing project that exposes U.S. government environmental data in plain view. Our goal is to make complex datasets accessible, defensible, and accountable for communities, researchers, and policymakers.

Mission

We believe transparency is the foundation of accountability. By transforming raw EPA datasets into clear maps, dashboards, and news updates, we empower people to understand pollution risks and demand action.

Methodology

Every dataset we use is sourced from authoritative national systems such as the EPA TRI, ECHO, and NPL programs. We validate, transform, and document each step in a permanent ledger to ensure reproducibility and trust.

Vision

Pollution Near Me aims to become the most reliable platform for environmental accountability in the United States. By combining ceremony, clarity, and operational proof, we ensure that every map, snapshot, and article is sealed with integrity.
