About Pollution Near Me

Transparency and accountability for environmental data.

Our Purpose

Pollution Near Me is an open project to expose toxic sites, violations, and environmental risks. We provide a public-facing interface for awareness while documenting the build process in a private ledger.

Methodology

Data sources include EPA datasets, AQI APIs, and other verifiable environmental feeds. Every dataset is timestamped and reproducible.

Accountability

All changes are logged in a build ledger. No baselines are sealed until operational proof is verified.
