About Pollution Near Me

Pollution Near Me is a public-facing project that exposes U.S. government environmental data in full view, making complex datasets accessible and actionable for communities, researchers, and policymakers.

Mission

We believe transparency is the foundation of accountability. By surfacing EPA datasets, we empower the public to hold polluters and regulators to account.

Methodology

Every dataset we use is sourced from authoritative national systems such as the EPA TRI, ECHO, and NPL programs. We transform, validate, and document each dataset to ensure reproducibility and defensibility.

Vision

Pollution Near Me aims to become the most reliable platform for environmental accountability in the United States, combining clarity, ceremony, and operational proof to drive change.
